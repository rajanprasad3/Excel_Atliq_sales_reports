# 📊 AtliQ Hardware – Excel Sales & Finance Reports

---

## 📌 Project Overview

AtliQ Hardware is a global company that supplies computer hardware and peripherals to clients across multiple markets. This project focuses on building **Sales and Finance Reports** using **Microsoft Excel** to help the sales and finance teams evaluate performance, track targets, and make data-driven decisions.

Two key sales reports and three P&L financial reports were created to give AtliQ's leadership a clear picture of customer performance, market standing, and overall financial health across fiscal years and months.

---

## 🎯 Objective

**Sales Reports:**
- Evaluate individual customer net sales performance year over year
- Compare actual market sales against set targets to identify gaps and opportunities

**Finance Reports:**
- Assess Profit & Loss across fiscal years to track financial growth
- Break down P&L by markets to identify high and low performing regions
- Analyze monthly P&L trends to understand seasonal revenue patterns

---

## 📁 Reports Included

| Report | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| `Customer Performance Report.pdf` | Year-over-year net sales performance for each customer |
| `Market Performance vs Target Report.pdf` | Comparison of actual sales vs targets across all markets |
| `P&L Statement by Fiscal Year.pdf` | Annual Profit & Loss statement covering net sales, COGS, and gross margin |
| `P&L Statement by Markets.pdf` | P&L breakdown by individual country and market |
| `P&L Statement by Months.pdf` | Monthly P&L trend analysis across fiscal quarters |

---

## 📐 Key Metrics Covered

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Net Sales** | Total revenue after returns and discounts |
| **COGS** | Cost of Goods Sold |
| **Gross Margin** | Net Sales minus COGS |
| **Gross Margin %** | Profitability ratio per market and customer |
| **Target vs Actual** | Gap analysis between sales targets and actual performance |
| **YoY Growth %** | Year-over-year percentage change in net sales |

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- 📈 Most customers showed significant net sales growth from 2020 to 2021
- 🎯 Several markets fell short of their 2021 sales targets, highlighting areas needing strategic focus
- 💹 Gross margin percentage varies widely across markets — some regions are more profitable than others
- 📅 Q3 and Q4 consistently show higher revenue due to festive season demand
- 🌍 India and the USA are among the largest contributing markets by net sales volume

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- **Microsoft Excel** – Core tool for data analysis and report creation
- **Power Query** – Data extraction, transformation, and loading (ETL)
- **Power Pivot** – Data modeling and relationship building across tables
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Custom measures and calculated columns
- **Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts** – Dynamic summarization and visualization
- **Conditional Formatting** – Visual highlights for performance indicators
